5. **Risk Awareness**: Clear disclaimers throughout
6. **Value Proposition**: Focus on structured learning and support
7. **Social Proof**: Use numbers responsibly (2,000+ students, not inflated)
8. **Urgency**: Based on real batch limitations, not fake scarcity
9. **Clear Guarantees**: 30-day satisfaction, not profit guarantees
10. **Compliance**: Proper risk disclaimers and realistic claims
